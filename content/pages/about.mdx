I'm Lorenzo Santucci — a freelance full-stack developer and ML/AI engineer based in Italy. I build web applications, machine learning pipelines, and the occasional pixel-art world when the mood strikes.

My work sits at the intersection of solid engineering and creative problem-solving. I care about clean architecture, type safety, and shipping things that actually work in production. The stack changes depending on the problem, but TypeScript, Python, and a healthy distrust of magic are constants.

When I'm not writing code, I'm probably walking Totti — my dog, unofficial project manager, and the real star of this operation. He has strong opinions about deployment schedules and treats.

This site is both a professional platform and a small experiment in how far you can push a personal website before it becomes something else entirely. Two views, one content system, zero corporate jargon.

If you need something built, estimated, or debugged, the contact page is right over there. If you're just exploring, there's more to find than you might expect.
