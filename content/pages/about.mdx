## What I do

I help founders and businesses turn complex ideas into clear, usable digital products.

I build full-stack software, AI-powered features and internal tools end to end.

## How I work

My approach combines product thinking, design sensibility and engineering. I’m especially useful when a project is still messy: too many ideas, unclear priorities or workflows that don’t quite hold together.

I bring structure to that stage and turn it into systems, interfaces and product decisions that are practical, coherent and ready to be built.

## My background

My background is in Economics, which shaped the way I think about systems, incentives and decision-making. Over time, that evolved into building software where technical depth and usability need to work together.
